Family Scheduler

家庭向けの意思決定支援アプリ。
予定調整や役割分担を可視化し、日々の判断をサポートします。

Overview

Family Scheduler helps working families coordinate childcare and household responsibilities by visualizing schedules and suggesting practical solutions to conflicts.

It visualizes each family member's daily schedule on an easy-to-read timeline, helping parents coordinate childcare, commuting, work, and household tasks.

When scheduling conflicts occur, the app proposes practical alternatives such as changing the responsible person or adjusting the time.

Screenshots

Features

  • Daily timeline for every family member
  • Manage work, childcare and household schedules
  • Automatically detect scheduling conflicts
  • Suggest possible solutions
  • Flexible assignment rules for each task
